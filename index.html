1v1 Teambuilder

A 1v1 tool designed for 1v1 players

1v1, or pretty much any OM has a vastly different (I hope) playstyle compared to your standard tiers. 1v1 has a special quality to it as it's unlike any other OM. (bar 2v2) That means it should have a totally different approach to teambuilding.

That's why I have created 1v1 teambuilder, a personlized teambuilder just for the 1v1 metagame that can design 1 team at a time. Think of it as a programming IDE (Integrated Development Environment) for 1v1 players!

    What This Application Includes:
  • A section where all standard 1v1 sets reside (much cleaner than the Pokepaste)
  • A way to track your team's progression through the teambuilding process from start to finish
  • An integrated Threat Checklist that you can check off when you feel that you can eliminate the threat with a specific pokemon or more.
  • New classifications or sortings of pokemon that are user friendly which guide you when teambuilding.
  • Speed Tiers make sense in this version.
  • A sleek design that makes building a team an enjoyable experience.
  • You can use it as a web application or download it!
